## 📊 Project Overview

This Annual Live Births Dashboard provides comprehensive insights into birth patterns across Malaysia over 24 years (2000-2023). The dashboard helps understand demographic trends, regional variations, ethnic distributions, and gender ratios in live births.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

- **Microsoft Excel**
  - Pivot Tables for data aggregation
  - Pivot Charts for visualization
  - Slicers for interactive filtering
  - Conditional Formatting for highlighting trends
  - Formulas for calculations (SUMIFS, COUNTIFS, AVERAGE, etc.)
 
---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Track annual live birth trends over 24 years
- Analyze state-wise distribution of births
- Compare male vs female birth ratios
- Understand ethnic composition of births
- Identify regional patterns across Malaysia
- Provide insights for healthcare planning and policy

---

### Key Time Period
- **Date Range:** 2000 - 2023 (24 years)
- **Geography:** Malaysia (States and Regions)
- **Demographics:** Ethnicity, Gender

---

## 📑 Dashboard Components

### 1. Time Series Analysis
- **Total Births Over Time:** Line chart showing annual birth trends from 2000-2023
- **Year Selection:** Interactive filter for specific year analysis
- **Trend Identification:** Peak years, declining periods, anomalies

### 2. State-wise Distribution
- **Top 10 States:** Bar chart ranking states by birth count
- **State Comparison:** Visual comparison of birth volumes
- **Percentage Distribution:** Share of total births by state

### 3. Gender Analysis
- **Male vs Female Births:** Pie chart showing gender distribution
- **Gender Ratio:** Male to female birth ratio over time
- **Trend Analysis:** How gender balance has changed

### 4. Ethnicity Breakdown
- **Major Ethnic Groups:** Bumi/Malay, Chinese, Others
- **Ethnic Distribution:** Proportion of births by ethnicity
- **Trend Analysis:** Changes in ethnic composition over time

### 5. Regional Analysis
- **Region Categories:**
  - East Coast Region
  - East Malaysia
  - Federal Territories
  - Northern Region
  - Southern Region
- **Regional Comparison:** Birth volumes by region
- **Regional Trends:** How regions compare over time

### 6. Geographic Visualization
- **Map View:** Geographic distribution of births across Malaysia
- **State Highlighting:** Visual representation of birth density
